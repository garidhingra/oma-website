About One Meal Apart

One Meal Apart was founded and is led by Garima Dhingra, a clinical nutrition practitioner with over three years of professional experience in structured care environments.

She has supported more than 1100 individuals within doctor-led healthcare models, working across metabolic health, hormonal regulation, digestive support, and sustainable weight management.

Professional Background

Garima has worked extensively in structured clinical settings, supporting patients through personalized nutrition planning and long-term metabolic stabilization.

Her foundation includes internship training at Medanta Medicity, Gurgaon, along with continuous independent practice focused on physiology-led nutrition care.

Clinical Philosophy

One Meal Apart operates on a simple principle — health does not change through extremes, but through structure.

The focus remains on metabolic clarity, protein and fiber alignment, hormonal rhythm support, gut stability, and realistic lifestyle integration.

There is no crash dieting. No rigid restriction. No guilt-based coaching.

Instead, structured guidance designed to be sustainable.

Why “One Meal Apart”

The name reflects a core belief: meaningful health shifts begin with small, consistent decisions.

You are not years away from stability — you are often just one structured decision away.
