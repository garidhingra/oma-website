How It Works

What happens after I start the assessment?

Once you submit your details, we review your current health context and guide you toward the most appropriate structured pathway. You are not placed into a generic plan.

Will this work for my condition?

OMA supports individuals managing PCOS, thyroid imbalance, gut concerns and metabolic weight challenges. Care is adapted to your physiology, not a fixed template.

How personalized is the approach?

Every plan is designed based on your routine, preferences and clinical needs. The focus is on sustainability and structure rather than restriction.

Do I need to follow a strict diet?

No. The goal is to create a system that fits your life. You are guided toward consistency, not rigidity.

When do I speak with the team?

After your assessment, the next steps are shared clearly, including when and how your consultation or onboarding begins.
