Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an online consultation?

Yes. All consultations and structured care are conducted online, allowing flexible access from anywhere while maintaining clinical depth.

Is this focused only on weight loss?

No. While weight regulation may be a goal, the primary focus remains metabolic clarity, hormonal balance, gut stability, and long-term sustainability.

What happens after I book?

You complete a structured intake form followed by a clinical discussion. Your personalized nutrition plan is delivered the same day, with weekly progress alignment.

How often do we connect?

Structured care includes an initial consultation followed by weekly follow-up reviews. Additional calls may be scheduled when clinically required.

Is ongoing support available between reviews?

Yes. Structured communication access is available during working hours for clarification and accountability.

How is the nutrition plan delivered?

Your plan is shared in a clear, structured digital format designed for practical day-to-day implementation. Adjustments are made weekly based on progress.

Is this a crash diet program?

No. OMA does not promote extreme restriction. The approach is physiology-based, structured, and designed for sustainable health outcomes.
